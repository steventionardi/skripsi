 (Indonesian) Pasar Gede Harjonagoro telrletak jalan Urip Sumoharjo, Kelurahan Sudiroprajan, Kecamatan Jebres - Surakarta, dibangun Sinuhun Pakoe Boewono X 1930. Pasar didesain Thomas Karsten, arsitek Belanda. Arsitek Pasar Gede perpaduan gaya Eropa gaya tradisional. Dikarenakan musibah kebakaran 2000, Pasar Gede renovasi merubah aslinya. berdirinya, pasar diberlakukan sistem jual beli sewa toko berjualan. system itu. lagi, Pasar Gede Harjonagoro pasar bertingkat Indonesia. (English) Gede Market is stated on Urip Sumoharjo street, under district Sudiroprajan, Jebres - Surakarta, which was built by Sinuhun Pakoe Boewono in 1930. Designed this market is Thomas Karsten, an arsitect form Dutch; market has a combination between European and Traditional styles. In 2000, market was on fire. It was then renovated without changing its original style. market was first market thet sell and rent system for its kiosks and built in level in Indonesia.
